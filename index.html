Uncompromising Safety Standards

At EXM, we prioritize the highest standards of safety and quality assurance. Each of our products undergoes the following rigorous processes:

Comprehensive Testing

Our products are subjected to multiple rounds of testing that exceed FDA requirements to ensure optimal safety and reliability.

14-Day Quarantine

All products undergo a 14-day quarantine period to mitigate the risk of bacterial and fungal contamination, ensuring they meet stringent health standards.

Advanced Disease Testing

We perform comprehensive testing to detect a range of potential diseases, including:

  • Hepatitis B & C
  • HIV-1 & HIV-2, HTLV I/II
  • Cytomegalovirus (CMV), Syphilis (RPR)
  • West Nile Virus (WNV)
  • Nucleic Acid Testing (NAT), including Ultrio testing for HIV, HCV, and HBV

These steps are a testament to our unwavering commitment to safety and the well-being of our customers.

What Makes EXM Therapy Unique?

Revive Stem’s EXM Therapy utilizes a highly advanced allograft that is minimally manipulated, preserving the full regenerative potential of its cellular components. This allograft includes a carefully balanced blend of key regenerative elements that work synergistically to support tissue repair and regeneration:

Mesenchymal Stem Cells

Versatile cells capable of differentiating into various tissue types, aiding in comprehensive tissue healing.

Progenitor Cells

Vital for initiating and sustaining tissue regeneration, accelerating recovery from injury or damage.

Exosomes

Nanoparticles that carry bioactive molecules to promote cell communication and enhance tissue regeneration at the molecular level.

Growth Factors & Proteins

Essential biomolecules that stimulate cell division, repair, and the regeneration of damaged tissues.

Perinatal PRP

Platelet-rich plasma sourced from the perinatal period, offering a potent mix of growth factors that accelerate healing and reduce inflammation.

Together, these elements form a powerful cellular matrix, creating a dynamic and supportive microenvironment that maximizes the body’s ability to heal naturally and effectively.
